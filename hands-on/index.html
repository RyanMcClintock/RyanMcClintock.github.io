About Knob Hill Studios

Welcome to Knob Hill Studios, a creative hub for woodworking, design, and rapid prototyping. Whether you're looking for custom furniture or prototype production, we're here to help.

We are passionate about creating functional and beautiful designs, leveraging state-of-the-art technology, traditional craftsmanship, and a keen eye for detail. Feel free to explore our work and reach out if you’d like to collaborate.

For more inspiration, visit Wood Magazine for tips and trends in the woodworking world.

Our Services

Woodworking

We craft bespoke furniture using high-quality wood. From cabinets to tables and chairs, or even end-grain cutting boards, we bring your ideas to life with meticulous attention to detail.

Design and Prototyping

With tools like our 100W CNC laser cutter and 3D printers, we offer rapid prototyping and production for your creative projects. Whether you’re an inventor, artist, or hobbyist, we provide the resources to turn concepts into reality.
